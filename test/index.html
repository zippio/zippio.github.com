About Us

We solve 3 challenges in Emerging Markets:

For buyers

76%* of prospective buyers of virtual currency and prepaid credit in Latin America don’t have a credit card. Zippio allows them to buy those products and services with cash, from a reseller friend.

* Euromonitor, 2010, SuperData Research, 2010, ZIPPIO estimates.

For users

Zippio users are registered resellers that sell to their friends a portion of their virtual credits and/or electronic money balances for cash. Resellers engage their friends (buyers) into a peer-to-peer transaction faciliated by Zippio’s mobile technology.
